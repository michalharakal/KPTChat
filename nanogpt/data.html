Training data

For educational purposed a simple data set is used for training. It is called tiny shakespaere and consists from 40,000 lines of Shakespeare from a variety of Shakespeare’s plays. It was featured in Andrej Karpathy’s blog post 'The Unreasonable Effectiveness of Recurrent Neural Networks': karpathy.github.io/2015/05/21/rnn-effectiveness/

Its a simple text file, so it can so also loaded.
